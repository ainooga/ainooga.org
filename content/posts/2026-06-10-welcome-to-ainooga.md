## Why AI Nooga?

Chattanooga has everything it needs to be a hub for thoughtful AI development: gigabit fiber, a thriving innovation district, world-class research at UTC and Vanderbilt just up the road, and a culture that values doing things together, in person.

What we've been missing is a community that brings it all together.

**AI Nooga** fills that gap. We're a club — not a company, not a consultancy, not a conference series. A club for people who want to understand AI deeply, shape how it's governed, and build real relationships with the people doing the work.

### Our pillars

**Education** — Hands-on workshops like our upcoming [Train a Language Model from Scratch](/events/train-llm-from-scratch). Study groups for papers. Office hours for people getting started.

**Policy** — AI policy is being written now, in Chattanooga and Nashville and Washington. We want the people making those decisions to hear from people who actually understand the technology. We'll host policy salons, write position papers, and engage with local government.

**Research** — Collaborative projects, journal clubs, connecting UTC researchers with industry practitioners.

**Networking** — Socials, mixers, mentorship pairings. AI is a team sport.

### Face to face

AI Nooga is fundamentally an in-real-life club. The website exists to get you to events, conversations, and relationships. We meet in person, in Chattanooga, in rooms where you can see the whites of someone's eyes while you argue about attention mechanisms.

If you're in Chattanooga and curious about AI — whatever your background — join us.
